About Us

InstiEase is a website which provides the students of IIT BHU a platform for managing their daily requirements like:

  • Mess
  • Healthcare
  • Laundry
  • Complaints

It saves the time of both the student and the administration as most features are provided at the same platform. Both students and staff are provided with the feature of user-authentication. There are more than ten hostels present in our campus, and InstiEase aims to provide facilities to all students residing in them and make management much more convenient. Our client is the student parliament, which has given us the permission to create a website which caters to the needs of students in such day to day activities.
